About BrowseBuddy

BrowseBuddy is a pixel pet that lives in your browser. It reacts to what you browse, helps you find deals with cashback, and keeps you company across the web.

Version: 1.0.0

Permissions used:

  • activeTab / tabs: To detect page context and incognito mode
  • storage: To save your pet state, coins, and preferences
  • alarms: For streak tracking and periodic tasks
  • notifications: For streak reminders and level-up alerts

Privacy Policy

BrowseBuddy respects your privacy:

  • All browsing detection is performed locally in your browser
  • No browsing data, URLs, or page content is sent to any server
  • No personal information is collected
  • Only affiliate conversion events are recorded (for reward crediting)
  • You can view and clear all stored data anytime in the Privacy tab
  • Extension works fully offline after installation

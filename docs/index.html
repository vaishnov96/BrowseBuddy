What BrowseBuddy does

A small set of useful features that stay out of your way until you need them.

📖

Page Summaries

Automatically reads the page you're on and gives you a quick summary — what it's about, how long it is, and what to expect.

⏱

Focus Timer

Set a Pomodoro-style timer right from the pet menu. A floating badge tracks your time so you can stay focused.

🎮

Mini Games

Take a break with a quick coin-catch game. Earn BuddyCoins and XP to level up your pet and unlock new things.

🎯

Context Aware

BrowseBuddy knows if you're watching YouTube, shopping, coding, or reading news — and reacts naturally to what you're doing.

🐾

Teach Tricks

Teach your buddy to sit, shake, roll over, and more. Unlock new tricks as your pet levels up through daily interaction.

📸

Screenshot Buddy

Capture a screenshot of any page with one click. Your buddy adds a quick camera flash for fun.

How it works

1

Install the extension

Add BrowseBuddy from the Chrome Web Store. Takes about 5 seconds.

2

Browse normally

Your pixel buddy appears in the corner of every page. It walks around and reacts to what you're doing.

3

Interact when you want

Click the pet to open the menu. Set timers, play games, read page summaries, or just say hi.

What BrowseBuddy does

A small set of useful features that stay out of your way until you need them.

📖

Page Summaries

Automatically reads the page you're on and gives you a quick summary — what it's about, how long it is, and what to expect.

🛒

Smart Shopping

Review summaries, price history tracking, coupon detection, and deal alerts on Amazon and Shopee.

⏱

Focus Timer

Set a Pomodoro-style timer right from the pet menu. A floating badge tracks your time so you can stay focused.

🎮

Mini Games

Take a break with a quick coin-catch game. Earn BuddyCoins and XP to level up your pet and unlock new things.

🐾

Teach Tricks

Teach your buddy to sit, shake, roll over, and more. Unlock new tricks as your pet levels up through daily interaction.

🎯

Context Aware

Knows if you're watching YouTube, shopping, coding, or reading news — and reacts naturally to what you're doing.
